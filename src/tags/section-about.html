About Us

PHPDetroit is a two-day, regional PHP conference that brings the community together to learn and grow. We're preceeding the conference with a 2 track tutorial day that will feature 4 sessions covering various topics.

We welcome developers and enthusiasts of all skill levels to come join us while we discuss the latest trends and technologies in our industry.

What is Our Goal?

PHPDetroit started as a small meetup group located in the Metro Detroit area. We've since grown to nearly 500 members, with experience ranging from first time developers, to seasoned veterans with decades of experience.

Our goal is to bring together like-minded PHP developers in an effort to network with our peers, learn new skills, and teach others. We welcome you to join us in our journey.
